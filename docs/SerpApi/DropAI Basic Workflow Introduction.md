### My Dropshipping Product Research Workflow

**1. Identify Product Trends**
Analyze current market trends to understand which products are gaining attention and demand.

**2. Discover Trending Products in Wholesale Markets**
Search for these trending products on wholesale platforms such as AliExpress, Alibaba, and other wholesale marketplaces.

**3. Collect Supplier Information**
Once the product is identified on wholesale platforms, gather detailed information about the suppliers.

**4. Analyze the Competitive Market**
Search for the exact same product on competitive selling platforms where dropshippers typically sell (such as Shopify stores and other e-commerce platforms).

**5. Collect Seller and Pricing Data**
Identify existing sellers and analyze the exact price points at which the product is currently being sold in the market.

**6. Perform Gap and Profit Analysis**
Compare the wholesale prices against the market selling prices to identify market gaps, business opportunities, and potential profit margins.
